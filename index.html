Welcome to 2b2f

2b2f is a lawless, chaotic Minecraft server running on version 1.12.2, the pinnacle of PvP mechanics and survival gameplay. There are no rules, admins, or restrictions. Anything is allowed on this server, there are no rules. Whether you want to create breathtaking builds, forge alliances, wage wars, or destroy everything in your path, 2b2f offers an unparalleled anarchy experience.

Explore a world scarred by battles, rich in player history, and filled with monumental builds. Survive, conquer, or destroy—the choice is yours.
